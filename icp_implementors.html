The 6-kit journey

Six kits. Four documents.
One structural foundation.

Each kit produces a working document. They compound: what you discover in Kit 1 shapes what Kit 2 asks. The journey produces architecture to operate from — not insight to reflect on.

Build status — stated accurately
Prompt architecture is structured for all six kits. The interface is in active development — not complete. Founding Implementors book orientation now; the kit journey begins when the interface is ready, Q2 2026.
01
Kit 1 · Core · Start here
Brand Inner Compass
Not the polished story — the real one. Why this business exists in this form, serving these people, right now. Purpose specific enough to say no to an opportunity is real. Everything else is aspiration.
Output: Brand Compass Document
02
Kit 2 · Core
VMV in Practice
Most VMV exercises produce words that sound right and make decisions poorly. If your VMV cannot resolve a hard trade-off, it needs rewriting until it can.
Output: Operational VMV Document
03
Kit 3 · Core · Most uncomfortable
Business Model Reality Check
Not the pain you assume customers have. The pain they will pay to solve right now. This kit asks whether the business model actually supports the stated purpose — or quietly contradicts it. The discomfort is where the structural truth lives.
Output: Economic Truth Summary
04
Kit 4 · Execution
Brand Strategy & Positioning
Customers buy outcomes. This kit closes the gap between what you believe your brand says and what the market actually hears — and produces the words that filter in the right people and out the wrong ones.
Output: Messaging Framework
05
Kit 5 · Execution
Decision Architecture
Maps the decision-making system of the venture — who decides what, how, and with what authority. Most businesses have implicit decision structures that actively contradict the stated values.
Output: Decision Architecture Map
06
Kit 6 · Operations
Operations Alignment
The final kit connects strategy to daily operations. Are the systems, team structure, and operational rhythms aligned with what the first five kits defined?
Output: Operations Alignment Map
