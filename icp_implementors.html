ICP is not a programme. It is a standard. A standard is not consumed — it is applied. The right founder evaluates it against their business and determines whether the architecture fits. This is that evaluation.

Most founders believe they have clarity. They have ambition. Ambition without architecture produces effort — not compound growth. The structural gap is almost always older than the symptoms that surface it.

If you are looking for coaching or emotional support — this is not it. ICP operates from honesty and structural rigour. That is not a limitation. It is the operating mode.

Who this is — and isn't — for

Clarity through exclusion.

This is the right path if
  • You think in systems and can tolerate structural honesty about your own business.
  • Your business is revenue-generating and at least 12 months operational. The kits require a real context to work on.
  • You suspect the root problem is structural — not marketing, not hiring, not another tool.
  • You're ready to name what's actually wrong — not what you'd prefer to be wrong.
This is not the right path if
  • You want validation of decisions already made. ICP will not provide that.
  • You're looking for immediate tactical outputs — a new website, a campaign, a rebrand.
  • You want a light advisory touch. This requires active participation across 6 months.
  • Your business is pre-revenue or early ideation. The kits require a real operating context.
  • You need to feel good about where the business is before you'll look at where it isn't.
The operating mode
ICP does not offer comfort designed to build trust. Honesty and structural integrity are the operating modes. What you receive is an accurate picture of your business — not a flattering one, and not a harsh one. An accurate one.
"This is not a programme designed to be comfortable. It is designed to be structurally honest — which is a different thing entirely."
The 6-kit journey

Six kits. Four documents.
One structural foundation.

Each kit produces a working document. They compound: what you discover in Kit 1 shapes what Kit 2 asks. The journey produces four architecture documents — not insight to reflect on, but structure to operate from.

Build status — stated accurately
Prompt architecture is structured for all six kits. The interface is in active development — not complete. Founding Implementors book orientation now; the kit journey begins when the interface is ready, Q2 2026. You are entering at v0.4. That is what the Founding Rate reflects.
Kit 1 · Core · Start here
Brand Inner Compass
Not the polished story — the real one. Why this business exists in this form, serving these people, right now. Purpose specific enough to say no to an opportunity is real. Everything else is aspiration.
Output: Brand Compass Document
Kit 2 · Core
VMV in Practice
Most VMV exercises produce words that sound right and make decisions poorly. If your VMV cannot resolve a hard trade-off, it needs rewriting until it can.
Output: Operational VMV Document
Kit 3 · Core · Most uncomfortable
Business Model Reality
Not the pain you assume customers have. The pain they will pay to solve right now. This is the kit founders resist most and benefit from most.
Output: Economic Truth Summary
Kit 4 · Core
Brand Strategy
Where in the market does this brand live — and why does that position belong to you specifically? The one-page output is the filter that makes every downstream decision faster.
Output: One-Page Brand Strategy
Kit 5 · Execution
Messaging Clarity
You talk about features. Customers buy outcomes. This kit closes that gap — and produces the words that filter in the right people and out the wrong ones.
Output: Messaging Framework
Kit 6 · Operations
Operations Alignment
The final kit connects strategy to daily operations. Are the systems, team structure, and operational rhythms aligned with what the first five kits defined?
Output: Operations Alignment Map
